Achievements are mini-tasks or goals that you can target while developing your game for extra bragging rights. Making a game is your goal here, and going for achievements is not required, but we hope that going for achievements will help you follow some best practices and get involved with the community at a deeper level. There are a total of 50 achievements that will be rolled out in increments of 10 on July 8th, 15th, and 22nd.

To log achievements, please post your team name and the name of the achievement at http://stlgamedev.slack.com/messages/gdd_achievements/. If you need an account, contact us through this form with your name, email address, and team name.
